2021 Version

Our old favourite music quiz – Name That Tune is back! Once again the quiz is only available by mail order.
Our chosen charity this year is The South Bucks Community Hospice. You can play in one of three ways:

HOW TO PLAY

1)     Buying a CD Pack
This year we will POST the pack to you by mail, including the CD and entry sheet.
Cost will be £6.50 (includes £1.00 to cover P&P)

2)     Buying a USB Memory Stick
We will post you the memory stick which includes all the tracks and an entry form.
Cost will be £7.50 (includes £1.00 to cover P&P)

3)     By email
We will email you a copy of all the clips as mp3 files, and an entry sheet. Your email must be capable of allowing approximately 10Mb of attachments.
Cost will be £5.50

HOW TO PAY

Please pay by PAYPAL if possible. 100% of the profit will be going to South Bucks Community Hospice Registered charity 1128881.
