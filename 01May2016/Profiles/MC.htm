MELBAGE CUP AND MGA PLAYER PROFILE

MARTIN COX - Founder Member


MARTIN COX

  

Martin Cox (Coxy) from London, England was one of the first and founding members of the Melbage Cup Golf Society. A popular and well respected member of the European Team. Has captained the European team on two occasions.

Badly injured in a freak accident in June 2002, he has recovered well and is now back playing again. 

An all round good guy with a lust for Diet Coke and crisps.
